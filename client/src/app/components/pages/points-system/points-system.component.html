Points System

Ordering points are Books World's swapping currency. The value of the books that are offered is given in points, and the points received for the books that have been sent, can again in return be swapped for the desired books. If you want to order a book, but don't have enough points, you can pay for the extra points during the ordering process (1 point = Â£1). Points cannot be exchanged for money. Credit is the equivalent of money. In case an order is cancelled, the money paid for the order will be refunded as Books World credit. Any available credit is automatically used when you make new orders.

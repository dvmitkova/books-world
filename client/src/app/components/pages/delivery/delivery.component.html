Delivery

Information for the sender

Properly packaging the books you are sending is important! We recommend reusing any existing materials you already have in your home - paper shopping bags, padded envelopes, boxes from previously received packages, etc. When you are packaging the books you send out, think about the recipient who is eagerly waiting for them and how upset they would be if they were damaged in transit.

Information for the recipient

After the sender has dropped off the book at the parcel machine, you will receive a notification email with the tracking code, which you can enter to track the parcel. Once your order has been posted, shipping usually takes 2-5 working days. Orders are delivered to your home address.
